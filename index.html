Hosting Canada

Hosting Canada is a hosting review website, which compiles and categorizes the details and benefits of many hosting providers, helping users to find the perfect hosting provider.

Learning the basics of regular expressions was pivotal while working with Hosting Canada. Using that knowledge, I was able to create a completely automated Table of Contents system. Paired with ACF, the product manager could turn on a table of contents on any page or post with one click, and even customize the headings in the table of contents should the automatically generated title not suit their needs.

American Marijuana

American Marijuana boasts many articles pertaining to a wide variety of marijuana-based products such as CBD, Cannabis, and accessories. Some articles are even backed by medical professionals to ensure scientific accuracy.

There is a robust tagging system in place for American Marijuana, categorizing a wide range of posts, compenets, etc. Using this tagging system, I built an Ad Management dashboard in the back-end of the site. The product manager was able to collect the ad scripts from the ad company, and simply paste them into the system. The tagging functionality allowed the product manager to add as many tags as they wished, associating specific scripts with that tag and ad size, which allowed extensive customization in ads and ad placement with little work.
